Spelling Rules

When forming the nominative plural of nouns, recall that if the stem ends in г, ж, к, х, ч, ш, or щ, you add и, not ы. This 7-letter spelling rule also applies to adjectives. This is why adjectives whose stem ends in one of these seven letters have the -ий ending instead of -ый. Four of these letters are also in the 5-letter spelling rule. This states that, if the stem ends in ж, ц, ч, ш, or щ, any unstressed o in an ending is instead written as e.
